Putin’s Big Loving Family of Clones is a hand-drawn comic realised for the finnish magazine KUTIKUTI, issue number 70 on conspiracy theories. The comic is inspired by the common belief in Putin’s numerous clones, it ironically portrays the dictator’s big family, as well as speculates on his loneliness and isolation. Coloured penicls, marker and liner.
