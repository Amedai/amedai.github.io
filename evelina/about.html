Ciao :)

I’m a Russian illustrator and graphic designer based in Bolzano, Italy. My practice is focused on contemporary publishing, illustration for children, as well as commercial illustration, comics and animation. I tend to experiment with my drawing techniques depending on the work, but I have a consistent flat representation and a passion for purposed disproportion. I used to work in vintage retail, so my characters tend to be well-dressed.
