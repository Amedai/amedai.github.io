Iosif and the Fog (italian: Iosif e la Nebbia) is an illustrated childrenâ€™s book about a lonely and unhappy dog Iosif, who travels to Italy and falls in love with the fog of the Venetian lagoon. The original story is inspired by by the figure of Iosif Brodskij, his personal relationship with Venice and his great work Watermark: an essay on Venice.

The book is the outcome of a semester illustration project Lagunar led by Geogio Camuffo, Andrea Fachetti and Marcello Barison. Realised in collaboration with We Are Here Venice in June 2022.
